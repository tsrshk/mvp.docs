# ADR-001 · LCOS is an invoice entry point, not a POS

**Status:** accepted · **Date:** 2026-07-02 (pre-existing)

## Context
There is a temptation to expand into operational accounting / POS.

## Decision
LCOS collects and analyzes data on top of the ERP; it is **not** a POS and **not** operational accounting. Tablet/POS integration and bookkeeping remain in the ERP (Esupl). Concretely, the system is an invoice write-point: photo → OCR → mapping to SKU → validation → Esupl payload → local save + (behind a gate) write to POS.

## Consequences
Payroll, shifts, financial reporting are out of scope. The product boundary is clear.
