# ADR-007 · Two independent authentication planes

**Status:** accepted · **Date:** 2026-07-02 (pre-existing, CLAUDE.md §11)

## Context
Mixing admin and user auth = a privilege-escalation risk.

## Decision
Two separate planes that do not mix:
- **App users** (`users`, argon2, JWT access + opaque refresh, cookies).
- **SQLAdmin operator** (env `ADMIN_USERNAME` + bcrypt, session cookie, **no row in `users`**).

## Consequences
Two sets of hash/verify; the operator is not provisioned in `users`. `admin_system` routes are gated by the SQLAdmin OPERATOR plane, not by the app-JWT superadmin.
