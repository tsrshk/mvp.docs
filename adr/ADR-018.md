# ADR-018 · SKU-identity commit-gate: POS = SoT, mapping on a durable id, variant A (block until confirmation)

**Status:** accepted · **Date:** 2026-07-08 (codification of [[DEC-0011]] + [[DEC-0013]]); veto of variant C — 2026-07-09

> This ADR **codifies** two detailed decisions. Full records: [[DEC-0011]] (POS as SoT + durable id + two-phase authority) and [[DEC-0013]] (commit requires confirmed identity — variant A).

## Context
Who owns ingredient identity, and how to commit a line with an exact-cache-match but without a confirmed `sku_mapping` — auto or block.

## Decision
Codifies **[[DEC-0011]]**: POS is the sole SoT of ingredient identity/attributes; `sku_mapping` is anchored to the durable `pos_ingredient_id`, **NOT** to the cache/surrogate PK; two-context resolution — draft tolerantly from the local catalog, commit fail-closed with live validation against POS.

Plus **[[DEC-0013]] variant A**: commit is allowed ONLY for a commit-eligible mapping (`method=manual` **OR** `confirmed_by IS NOT NULL`); cache exact / fuzzy / AI do **NOT** auto-commit and do **NOT** auto-create a mapping. The moat (`sku_mapping`) grows only through explicit human confirmation.

## Veto (2026-07-09, Ivan)
The `TZ__STABILIZATION_2026-07-09` proposal (D1 / S6) to revive **variant C** — where an exact-cache-match auto-creates a `sku_mapping` (`method='cache_exact'`, `confirmed_by='system'`) and commits — is **rejected**. Reason: variant C ranks below DEC-0013 in authority, rolls back the fail-closed invariant, and breaks the merge_gate test `test_exact_cache_match_does_not_commit_and_creates_no_mapping`. The single resolution integration point is `sku_mapping` only; the cache is absent from the commit path.

## Consequences
- Every new item requires a one-time manual confirmation (this is the "review").
- The mapping moat survives a drop+rebuild of the cache.
- VER-022 (cache scope asymmetry) is closed — the cache is off the commit path.
- **Open: VER-021** (durability of `pos_ingredient_id`) — a GATE, requires a WRITE into the Esupl sandbox → owner-run, not closable in read-only.
