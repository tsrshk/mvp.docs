# ADR-017 · Supplier self-service — leave the door open, don't build the portal

**Status:** accepted · **Date:** 2026-07-03

## Context
In the future a supplier fills in their own settings (schedule, minimum order amount, price list), but building a portal in Phase 1 would spread focus thin (solo developer, "one routine at a time").

## Decision
Lay down only the schema groundwork, not behavior:
- a `supplier` value in the `Role` enum,
- a nullable `suppliers.portal_user_id → users.id`,
- settings in a separate `supplier_settings` table (the very one the supplier later edits themselves).

No routes, no UI, no branches in auth. Deferred: a global supplier user, invite tokens, an access scope of "only own settings".

## Consequences
The enum/FK migration is cheap now, expensive later; `suppliers` remains a mirror of Esupl, while terms live separately.
