# ADR-002 · Human-in-the-loop

**Status:** accepted · **Date:** 2026-07-02 (pre-existing)

## Context
The owner's trust in the AI and the risk of an erroneous automatic action.

## Decision
The AI shows data and arguments — **the human decides**. No auto-orders; no write to POS without human confirmation.

## Consequences
Writing to the ERP is always behind a confirmation; the UI must provide sanity checks.
