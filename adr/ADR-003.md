# ADR-003 · Phase discipline and the Pilot-Gate (Wife-Gate)

**Status:** accepted · **Date:** 2026-07-02 (pre-existing)

> **Terminology:** Wife-Gate == **Pilot-Gate** — this is one and the same event. In the new documentation the name **Pilot-Gate** is preferred, referencing this ADR.

## Context
The classic trap of "building for the market — nobody buys."

## Decision
Phase 1 is a personal tool for Customer Zero (the wife, a coffee-shop owner). The transition to Phase 2 is gated by the **Pilot-Gate** criterion: the wife herself says "it's worse without it" + ≥3 h/week saved after 4 weeks of use. "First help the wife. Then — money."

## Consequences
Commercial ambition is an option, not an obligation; no premature market pressure. E9–E15 (Phase 2) open only after passing the Pilot-Gate.
