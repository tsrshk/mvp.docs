# ADR-006 · Fail-closed everywhere

**Status:** accepted · **Date:** 2026-07-02 (pre-existing)

## Context
Silent degradation is more dangerous than an explicit failure.

## Decision
Absence/unavailability of a dependency → hard error, not degradation:
- VPN down (when `ai_vpn_enabled`) → `VpnUnavailableError`, no silent direct egress.
- No AI key → `AiUnavailableError`.
- No POS token → Esupl 401.
- Ciphertext with an empty keyring → `RuntimeError`.
- `erp_write_enabled` defaults to `False`.

## Consequences
No "backup paths"; predictability over availability.
